  2. Day 2 (Sun) — Coordination, d & f-Block → Set 05, 06 + full KCET 2024 paper timed.

  4. Day 4 (Tue) — Equilibrium, GOC, Chem Bonding, Biomolecules, Redox → 04, 11.
  5. Day 5 (Wed) — Hydrocarbons, Thermo, Atom, Periodicity, Mole (flashcard pass). Re-do KCET 2025 under strict 80-min timer. Sleep by 10 pm.

How to use

  1. Timer first. Open the practice set, start an 80-sec × 10 = 13-min timer. Attempt without looking at answers.
  2. 30-sec rule. If you can't see the approach within 30 sec, circle and move on. Come back at the end.
  3. Error log. Every wrong question → write the correct concept on a sticky note. Stick it where you'll see it.
  4. Review Day 5 evening. Just scan the stickies. Not the textbook. Not the solutions. Only your own mistakes.
