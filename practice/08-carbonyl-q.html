Aldehydes, Ketones & Carboxylic Acids — 10 MCQs

Target time: 13 min · Volatile chapter (10 Q across 2022–2025; 0 in 2024 → 5 in 2025)
Key reactions:
  • Tollens' (ammoniacal AgNO₃): aldehyde → Ag mirror; Fehling's (alk Cu²⁺): aliphatic CHO only → red Cu₂O ppt
  • Cannizzaro: 2 R-CHO (no α-H) + NaOH → R-CH₂OH + RCOONa; Aldol: 2 R-CHO (has α-H) + dil NaOH → β-hydroxy aldehyde
  • Clemmensen (Zn-Hg/HCl) & Wolff-Kishner (N₂H₄/KOH, glycol): C=O → CH₂; Rosenmund: R-COCl + H₂/Pd-BaSO₄ → R-CHO
  • HVZ: R-CH₂-COOH + X₂ / red P → R-CHX-COOH; Acidity: HCOOH > CH₃COOH; EW group (Cl, NO₂) ↑ acidity; EDG (CH₃) ↓
