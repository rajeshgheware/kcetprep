Chemical Kinetics — 10 MCQs

Target time: 13 min · Core chapter (14 Q across 2022–2025)
Key formulas:
  • First order: k = (2.303/t) log (a / (a − x)); t1/2 = 0.693/k (independent of [A]0)
  • Zero order: [A] = [A]0 − kt; t1/2 = [A]0/(2k)
  • Arrhenius: k = A e−Ea/RT; log(k2/k1) = (Ea/2.303R)(T2−T1)/(T1T2)
  • Units of k: zero → mol L⁻¹ s⁻¹; 1st → s⁻¹; 2nd → L mol⁻¹ s⁻¹
