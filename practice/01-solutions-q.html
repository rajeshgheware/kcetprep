Target time: 13 min · Core chapter (16 Q across 2022–2025)
Key formulas:
  • Raoult's law (non-volatile solute): P = xsolvent · P°
  • ΔTb = iKbm · ΔTf = iKfm · π = iCRT (C in mol/L, T in K)
  • Henry: p = KH·x · KH increases with temperature (solubility decreases)
  • Van 't Hoff factor i = (observed) / (theoretical); i>1 dissociation, i<1 association
