Equilibrium — 10 MCQs

Target time: 13 min · Core chapter (10 Q across 2022–2025)
Key formulas:
  • Kp = Kc(RT)Δn where Δn = moles gaseous products − reactants
  • pH = −log [H⁺]; Henderson (buffer): pH = pKa + log ([salt]/[acid])
  • For weak acid HA: [H⁺] = √(Ka × C); Kw = Ka × Kb = 10⁻¹⁴
  • Ksp(AxBy) = xx yy sx+y (s = molar solubility)
