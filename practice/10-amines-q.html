Amines — 10 MCQs

Target time: 13 min · Core chapter (11 Q across 2022–2025)
Key reactions:
  • Hofmann bromamide: R-CONH₂ + Br₂/4NaOH → R-NH₂ + Na₂CO₃ + 2NaBr (one C less); only 1° amines
  • Gabriel synthesis: only 1° amines (aromatic amines can't be made this way); Carbylamine (isocyanide) test: only 1° amines with CHCl₃ + alc KOH
  • Diazotisation: Ar-NH₂ + HNO₂ (NaNO₂ + HCl) at 0–5 °C → Ar-N₂⁺ Cl⁻ (aliphatic diazonium is unstable and decomposes)
  • Basicity in water (aqueous): 2° > 1° ≈ 3° > NH₃ (aliphatic); aniline < NH₃ (lone pair delocalised into ring); EDG on para increases basicity, EWG decreases
