d & f-Block Elements — 10 MCQs

Target time: 13 min · Core chapter (13 Q across 2022–2025)
Key recalls:
  • Spin-only magnetic moment: μ = √(n(n+2)) BM (n = unpaired electrons)
  • Anomalous configs: Cr = [Ar]3d⁵4s¹, Cu = [Ar]3d¹⁰4s¹ (half-filled/full d more stable)
  • KMnO₄ in acidic: Mn⁷⁺ → Mn²⁺ (5 e⁻); K₂Cr₂O₇ in acidic: Cr₂O₇²⁻ → 2Cr³⁺ (6 e⁻ for 2 Cr)
  • Lanthanoid contraction: steady ↓ in ionic radius across Ln series; causes 2nd & 3rd transition series to have nearly equal atomic radii (e.g. Zr ≈ Hf)
