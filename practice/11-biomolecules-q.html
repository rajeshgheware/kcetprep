Biomolecules — 10 MCQs

Target time: 13 min · Core chapter (10 Q across 2022–2025)
Key recalls:
  • Glucose: aldohexose, 5 -OH + 1 -CHO, forms pentaacetate, oxidises to gluconic acid (Br₂/water) or saccharic acid (HNO₃)
  • Sucrose: α-D-glucose + β-D-fructose (α,β-1,2 glycosidic bond) → non-reducing; maltose & lactose are reducing
  • Starch = amylose (linear α-1,4) + amylopectin (α-1,4 + α-1,6 branches); cellulose = β-1,4 glucose
  • DNA: deoxyribose + A-T, G-C; RNA: ribose + A-U, G-C; Peptide bond = -CO-NH- between amino acids
