Non-negotiables

  • Sleep 7+ hrs every night. One late night crushes next-day recall. Non-optional.
  • No new material from Day 5 evening onwards. Only error log + formula sheets.
  • KCET has no negative marking. Never leave a question blank — even a 25% guess is +EV.
  • Time discipline. 60 Qs in 80 min = 80 sec/Q. If stuck >45 sec, mark and move. Return at end.
  • Don't discuss papers between exams. Between Physics AM and Chemistry PM on Thu, stay off phone / group chats. Comparing answers destroys confidence for the next paper.
  • Hydration + light meals on exam days. Heavy food before exam = slower brain.
  • Admit card, photo ID, pens, transparent bottle — pack the night before.
