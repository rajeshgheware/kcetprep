What the 8-year data confirms

The Core 10 are rock solid. Each appears 22–28 times across 8 years. Solutions, Electrochem, Kinetics, d/f-Block, Coordination each deliver ≥3 Q/yr in 7 of 8 years.
Shift in organic focus: Aldehydes/Ketones averaged 3.5/yr in 2018–21 but 2.5/yr in 2022–25. Alcohols/Phenols went the opposite way (2.5 → 3.5). Prep both equally — combined they're ~6 Q/yr.
Haloalkanes had a zero in 2023 but 2.75 avg across 8 years — came back strong. Don't be spooked by single-year zeros.
2025 had 60/60 in-syllabus; earlier years had 40–47/60 retained. The 2026 paper will almost certainly be 60/60 retained too.

Tactics (how to study)

  1. Inorganic = NCERT line-by-line. d/f-Block & Coordination questions often lift statements verbatim from NCERT. Memorise, don't over-analyse.
  2. Organic = reaction trees per functional group. Single-page "start → product" grids for Alcohols, Carbonyls, Haloalkanes, Amines. 80% of organic Qs test transformations, not full mechanisms.
  3. Physical = formula sheet + 3 solved examples per topic. Solutions, Electrochem, Kinetics, Equilibrium formulas all fit on one A4.
  4. Mock cadence: 80 min / 60 Q. 30-sec rule: if no approach, circle and move. Come back at the end.
  5. Error log: every wrong question → write the correct concept on a sticky note. Day-5 evening revision = scan stickies.
